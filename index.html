Welcome to the Sage X3 Internet of Things Hackathon

This 2-day FREE hackathon provides you with a team of developers, architects, analysts, entrepreneurs and technologists the challenge to create applications of the future.

Developing on the Sage X3 platform and using the Sage X3 APIs, teams will create smart, connected business solutions, applications and processes of the future in a fun and exciting event.

Here we'll show you how to develop on the IoT-ready Sage X3 platform through Examples, Workshops and Webinars. You will find the documents you need to get started!

IoT kits and equipment will be provided. Feel free to bring you own too. We look forward to seeing you there.

ELIGIBILITY Participants: Individuals (over 18 years of age) Locations: Newcastle, Manchester, Reading, Dublin

CATEGORIES •Smart Manufacturing - Continuously monitor critical assets, equipment and processes to proactively identify issues before they disrupt production. •Connected Products and After Sales Service - Monitor products, their location and performance to proactively take action in the field. •Connected Supply Chain - Keep track of inbound and outbound information, timely order fulfilment and critical in-transit parameters such as temperature to ensure optimal performance. •Real-time financial data capture, analysis and reporting

Produce real-time applications using sensors and devices connected to the Sage X3 IoT-ready platform Design and develop business processes, analytics and dashboards to visualise production data.
